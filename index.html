About Me

I’m Sridharani Gunasekaran, a certified professional makeup artist passionate about creating radiant, timeless looks that enhance natural beauty. My expertise spans bridal, editorial, and fashion makeup — with a focus on flawless, camera-ready finishes.

Every look I design is customized to match the client’s personality, occasion, and comfort. My goal is to make you feel confident, beautiful, and effortlessly elegant.

Makeup is not about changing faces, it’s about celebrating them.
